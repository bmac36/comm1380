Presenting Information and Social Media

Activity: Describing Primary Research


Best Practice

To show that you’ve done your primary research, you want to provide specific examples of how different social media sites might benefit your employer and also cite credible resources on the topic. If there are terms that are specific to a certain social media platform, make sure you clearly explain them so that your reader can follow.

Between the reading and the content found here, we’ve covered the different parts of an informal report. You should also be familiar with different types of social media. If you’re going to write a report on different social media sites for your employer, you’ll need to show that you have done the proper research to ensure your report is credible. This means you’ll need to describe research you’ve conducted on your own.

Part of doing research is providing the proper context for your sources and presenting your information in a way that your readers can easily follow along and understand your conclusions or recommendations.
